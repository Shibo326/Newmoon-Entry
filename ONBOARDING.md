# NightScore — User Onboarding Guide

## Try NightScore in 2 Minutes

NightScore is a privacy-preserving credit scoring DApp on Midnight. You can prove your DeFi creditworthiness without revealing any wallet data.

### Prerequisites
- [Lace Wallet](https://www.lace.io/) browser extension installed
- Lace connected to Midnight Preprod network
- Some tNIGHT tokens (free from faucet)

### Step 1: Get tNIGHT Tokens (Free)
1. Open Lace wallet
2. Switch to Midnight Preprod network
3. Copy your wallet address
4. Visit the [Preprod Faucet](https://faucet.preprod.midnight.network/)
5. Paste your address and request tokens

### Step 2: Connect to NightScore
1. Visit [https://newmoon-entry-projects.vercel.app](https://newmoon-entry-projects.vercel.app)
2. Click "Connect Lace Wallet"
3. Approve the connection in your Lace wallet popup

### Step 3: Compute Your Credit Score
1. Enter your 6 private signal values (these NEVER leave your device):
   - Wallet Age (days)
   - TX Frequency
   - DeFi Interactions
   - Repayment History (%)
   - Asset Diversity
   - Liquidation Events
2. Click "Compute Credit Score"
3. Wait for the ZK proof to generate (~30 seconds)
4. Your weighted score appears — proven on-chain without revealing your inputs

### Step 4: Verify a Threshold
1. Scroll to "Threshold Verification"
2. Enter a score and select a minimum grade (AAA, AA, A, BBB, BB, C)
3. Click "Verify Threshold"
4. The result is ONLY true/false — the verifier never sees your actual score

### Step 5: Leave Feedback
1. Scroll to the feedback form at the bottom
2. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
3. Select a category
4. Write your thoughts
5. Submit — thank you!

## What's Private vs Public

| Private (never on-chain) | Public (on-chain) |
|--------------------------|-------------------|
| Your 6 input signals | Computed score |
| Your wallet history | Registered flag |
| Your actual grade | Threshold result (true/false) |
| AI reasoning | Total scored counter |

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need real wallet data?**
A: No! You can enter any values to test the system. The privacy guarantee is that whatever you enter never appears on-chain — only the computed result does.

**Q: Is this on mainnet?**
A: No, this is on Midnight Preprod (testnet). tNIGHT tokens have no real value.

**Q: What happens to my data?**
A: Your 6 input signals are processed locally in a ZK circuit. Only the proof (not the inputs) goes on-chain. We never see or store your raw inputs.

**Q: How long does proof generation take?**
A: About 30 seconds on Preprod. This is the ZK proof being generated by the proof server.
